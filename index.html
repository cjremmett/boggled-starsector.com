    Terraforming Features:

    Terraform planets into one of six different types: Terran, water-covered, desert, jungle, tundra and frozen.

    Improve farming, organics and volatiles resources.

    Modify planetary weather patterns and climate.

    Build gigantic domed cities to protect your colonists from harsh surface conditions.

    Unleash gene-modded wildlife to attract tourists and generate revenue.

    Build a network of mass drivers to transport water-ice for terraforming purposes.

    Station Construction Features:

    Construct orbital habitats around your planets, each with their own market and building slots.

    Build mining stations to exploit asteroid belts.

    Build siphon stations to extract volatiles from the atmosphere of gas giants.

    Colonize existing abandoned stations to expand your faction in a resource-efficient manner.

    Miscellaneous Features:

    Discover Domain-era artifacts that can be used to build technologically-advanced marvels.

    Utilize AI cores to surveil your colonists and disrupt Pather cells.

    Train new AI cores using a Domain-era supercomputer.

    Build advanced comm relays, nav buoys and sensor arrays with increased bonuses.

    Place an AI core in charge of an automated battlestation to defend your colonies.
